Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Music Player ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being fully transparent about our practices.

Short version: Smart Music Player only accesses audio files stored on your device to provide playback functionality. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data with any third parties.

Data Access

What the App Accesses

Smart Music Player requests permission to access your device's local storage solely to read and play audio files you choose to open within the app.

  • Local audio and music files stored on your device
  • Embedded track metadata such as title, artist, and album art
  • No access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location
  • No access to files outside your media library
Data Collection

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • We do not upload your audio files to any server
  • We do not track your usage behavior or listening history
  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting tools that identify you personally
  • We do not serve advertisements or share data with advertising networks
Third Parties

Third-Party Services

Smart Music Player does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks that collect user data. All audio playback is handled entirely on your device.

Children

Children's Privacy

Smart Music Player is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since our app does not collect any personal data from any user, no additional measures are required.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

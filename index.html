Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Video Player ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being fully transparent about our practices.

Short version: Smart Video Player only accesses videos and images stored on your device to provide playback functionality. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data with any third parties.

Data Access

What the App Accesses

Smart Video Player requests permission to access your device's local storage solely to read and play video and image files you choose to open within the app.

  • Local video files stored on your device
  • Local image files stored on your device
  • No access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location
  • No access to files outside your media library
Data Collection

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • We do not upload your videos or images to any server
  • We do not track your usage behavior or viewing history
  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting tools that identify you personally
  • We do not serve advertisements or share data with advertising networks
Third Parties

Third-Party Services

Smart Video Player does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks that collect user data. All media playback is handled entirely on your device.

Children

Children's Privacy

Smart Video Player is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since our app does not collect any personal data from any user, no additional measures are required.
